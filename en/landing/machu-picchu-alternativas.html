Frequently asked questions

Common questions when tickets are unavailable

Can My Cusco Trip guarantee tickets if they are sold out?

We do not promise availability that does not officially exist. We review real options, nearby dates, available circuits and packages that may help you reorganize your trip.

Why does staying overnight in Aguas Calientes help?

Because it gives you more operational flexibility to coordinate trains, buses, schedules and possible alternatives without depending on a very tight full-day itinerary.

What if I already have confirmed flights?

We prioritize alternatives within your fixed dates. That is why we ask whether you have already purchased flights: it is an important sign of urgency and real travel intent.

Can I receive a personalized proposal?

Yes. Complete the form and we will contact you through your preferred channel with an alternative based on your situation.
