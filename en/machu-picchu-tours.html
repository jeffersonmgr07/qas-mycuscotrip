When should I book my Machu Picchu ticket?

It is best to book in advance, especially in high season, holidays or dates with high demand for trains and tickets.

What does a Machu Picchu tour include?

Depending on the option selected, it may include train, Consettur bus, Machu Picchu entrance, professional guide, transfers and assistance.

Is it better to visit Machu Picchu in one full day or 2 days?

Full day is practical if you have little time. The 2-day option lets you travel more calmly and sleep in Aguas Calientes.

Can I choose Expedition, Voyager or Vistadome train?

Yes. Availability and price difference depend on the date, schedule and railway company.
