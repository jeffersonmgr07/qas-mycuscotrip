When should I book my Machu Picchu ticket?

It is best to book in advance, especially during high season, holidays or dates with high demand for trains and entrance tickets.

What is included in a Machu Picchu tour?

Depending on the option you choose, it may include train tickets, Consettur bus, Machu Picchu entrance, a professional guide, transfers and local assistance.

Is it better to visit Machu Picchu as a full-day tour or a 2-day trip?

A full-day tour is practical if you have limited time. The 2-day option lets you travel at a more relaxed pace and spend the night in Aguas Calientes.

Can I choose Expedition, Voyager or Vistadome train service?

Yes. Availability and price differences depend on the date, schedule and train company.
