Which trek to Machu Picchu should I choose?

It depends on your fitness level, available days, budget and travel style. The Inca Trail is historic, Salkantay is scenic and Inca Jungle combines several adventure activities.

Do treks require advance booking?

Yes, especially the Inca Trail, because permits are limited and depend on official availability.

Do I need previous hiking experience?

Not always, but some routes require good physical condition. We review the difficulty and pace before recommending an option.
