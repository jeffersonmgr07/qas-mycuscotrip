Not a conventional tour or instant purchase

The traditional knowledge and uses of Ayahuasca practiced by Amazonian native communities have been declared Cultural Heritage of the Nation in Peru. This page does not sell a ceremony as an instant product: information is requested first and the case is referred for responsible assessment.

Participation must remain subject to the responsible facilitator’s criteria, informed consent and prior review of personal conditions, medications and expectations. It is not presented as a cure, guaranteed therapy or substitute for medical or psychological care.
