An experience blending science and Andean worldview

This experience combines an introduction to Inca and Andean astronomy, a presentation of the southern sky and a stargazing session. Depending on the selected option, it may include an immersive dome, cultural interpretation and telescopes.

Outdoor observation depends on weather, cloud cover and visibility. We therefore confirm operation and timing before the activity.
